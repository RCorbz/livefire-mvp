# User Personas

## 1. The Grunt (Coach Mike)
* **Role:** Head Coach, B2012 Premier.
* **Location:** Galena Park, Draper.
* **Pain Point:** Wasting Tuesday nights running cone drills that don't improve his players. Hates texting 10 coaches to find a scrimmage.
* **Behavior:** Uses phone on the field. Has dirty thumbs. Needs big buttons.
* **Goal:** Wants to "upgrade" his practice to a Joint Training session to look good to parents and improve players.

## 2. The Whale (Director Dave)
* **Role:** Director of Coaching (DOC).
* **Pain Point:** Liability and Brand Dilution. Terrified of his teams playing "Shadow Games" he doesn't know about.
* **Goal:** Control. Wants a dashboard to see where his teams are and who they are playing.
* **Trigger:** "European Academy Style" training.

## 3. The Skeptic (The Parent)
* **Role:** Pays the bills.
* **Goal:** Wants to know "Where is the field?" and "What time?" immediately.
* **Constraint:** Will NEVER download an app. Must access info via Web Link.
